Hyderabad

Hyderabad is a historic city known for its rich culture, famous monuments, and delicious food. Landmarks like Charminar, Golconda Fort, and Hussain Sagar reflect its royal past under the Nizams. The city is also famous for Hyderabadi biryani, Irani chai, and lively street markets that attract visitors from all over India.

Today, Hyderabad is one of the fastest growing IT hubs in the country. Areas like Hitech City and Gachibowli are filled with multinational companies and startups. Despite rapid modernization, the city still maintains its traditional charm, making it a perfect blend of old heritage and modern development.
